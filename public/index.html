What is CLV?

Customer lifetime value (CLV) is one of the key stats to track as part of a customer experience program. Customer lifetime value is a measurement of how valuable a customer is to your company, not just on a purchase-by-purchase basis but across entire customer relationships.

Why is CLV Important?

CLV is crucial for businesses because it enables them to:

  • Understand the long-term value of each customer
  • Allocate marketing resources more effectively
  • Improve customer retention strategies
  • Make data-driven decisions for pricing and customer segmentation
  • Enhance overall profitability by focusing on high-value customers
